# Pigeon Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

Last updated / 最后更新: 2026-05-14

---

## English

Pigeon is a local-first mood journaling app. Our principle is simple: **your data belongs to you and stays on your device.**

### What we collect

Pigeon has no accounts and requires no sign-up. Everything you create in Pigeon is stored on your device:

- **Photos** — Photos you capture are stored locally on your device.
- **Moods, notes, activity tags** — The mood you pick, optional text notes, and optional activity tags for each entry are stored locally on your device.
- **Location** — If you grant location permission, Pigeon records where a moment was captured, so it can show the place name and weather when you look back. Location data is stored locally on your device.

### How we use permissions

- **Camera** — To capture photos.
- **Location (When In Use)** — To tag where an entry was captured. This information is sent to Apple's weather service (WeatherKit) and geocoding service to display weather and place names — these are system services provided by Apple. You may deny location access; Pigeon's core features still work.
- **Add to Photos** — If you enable this option, Pigeon also saves captured photos to your system photo library.

### What we don't do

- We have **no servers** and **never** upload any of your data to the developer.
- We **do not** use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.
- We **do not** track you across apps or websites.

### Deleting your data

Your data is stored on your device. Deleting an entry in Pigeon also deletes its photo. Uninstalling Pigeon removes all of its local data.

### Children's privacy

Pigeon does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since Pigeon does not collect or upload any data, no such risk exists.

### Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same address with a new "last updated" date.

### Contact

Questions? Contact: dingtee@gmail.com

## 中文

Pigeon 是一款本地优先的情绪记录应用。我们的原则很简单:**你的数据属于你,留在你的设备上。**

### 我们收集什么

Pigeon 不设账号,不需要注册。你在 Pigeon 里产生的所有内容都存储在你的设备上:

- **照片** — 你拍摄的照片保存在设备本地。
- **情绪、笔记、活动标签** — 你为每条记录选择的情绪、可选的文字笔记和活动标签,存储在设备本地。
- **位置** — 在你拍摄时,如果你授权了定位权限,Pigeon 会记录拍摄地点,用于在回看时显示地名和天气。位置信息存储在设备本地。

### 我们如何使用权限

- **相机** — 用于拍摄照片。
- **位置(使用期间)** — 用于为记录标注拍摄地点。该信息会发送给 Apple 的天气服务(WeatherKit)和地理编码服务,以显示天气和地名——这些是 Apple 提供的系统服务。你可以拒绝定位权限,Pigeon 的核心功能不受影响。
- **添加到相册** — 如果你开启该功能,Pigeon 会把拍摄的照片同时保存到系统相册。

### 我们不做什么

- 我们**没有**服务器,**不会**把你的任何数据上传给开发者。
- 我们**不使用**任何第三方分析、广告或追踪服务。
- 我们**不会**跨应用或跨网站追踪你。

### 数据的删除

你的数据存储在设备上。在 Pigeon 内删除记录会同时删除对应的照片。卸载 Pigeon 会删除其全部本地数据。

### 儿童隐私

Pigeon 不会有意收集儿童的个人信息。由于 Pigeon 不收集、不上传任何数据,也不存在此类风险。

### 政策变更

若本政策有变更,更新后的版本会发布在同一网址,并标注新的"最后更新"日期。
